About Us


The VSLV Project (VSSUT Satellite Launching Vehicle), is a student initiative, in which a team of budding technocrats take on a herculean task of developing an Indigenous Sounding Rocket and PicoSAT, by venturing into Rocket Science, through a series of ten arduous missions. A one-of-its-kind, it is India’s First Indigenous Multipurpose Reusable Student Rocketry Project, that aims to build, launch, test and recover indigenously developed Launch Vehicles and Prototype Satellites in order to provide an educational experience to students in design, flight and data analysis of a space experiment. It is intended to prepare students for an exciting, technical challenge that may lead to launching space experiments into low earth orbits and beyond.
